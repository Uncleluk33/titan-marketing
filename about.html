Our Story

Started From the Field, Not a Boardroom

Titan Marketing Agency is based in Owings Mills, MD and founded by Luke Clahar — someone who owns and operates a local service business. That means we've personally experienced what it's like to run Google Ads with no clear results, pay an agency that doesn't explain anything, and wonder why the phone isn't ringing despite spending hundreds a month.

That firsthand experience is what drives everything we do. We built Titan Marketing to be the agency we wished we had — one that focuses on results you can actually measure: calls, leads, and booked jobs.

We work exclusively with local service businesses — towing companies, contractors, plumbers, HVAC providers, and similar trades. That focus means we understand your customers, your competition, and the keywords that actually convert in your market.

"If it's not getting you calls, it's not working. That's the only metric that matters." — Luke Clahar, Founder
