Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Titan Marketing Agency ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website titanadgrouphq.com and provides Google Ads management and digital marketing services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or engage our services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and business details when you reach out to us directly.
  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and referring URLs, collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Client account data — for clients who engage our services, we may access Google Ads account data, Google Business Profile data, and website analytics solely for the purpose of delivering our services.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide requested services.
  • To manage and optimize Google Ads campaigns on behalf of clients.
  • To send service updates, reports, and communications related to your account.
  • To improve our website and marketing materials.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Google Ads API & Platform Data

Titan Marketing Agency uses the Google Ads API to manage advertising campaigns on behalf of clients. Access to client Google Ads accounts is granted explicitly by each client and used solely to provide campaign management services. We do not share, sell, or use client advertising data for any purpose outside of delivering agreed-upon services.

Our use of Google Ads API and related Google services complies with Google's API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data including pages viewed, session duration, and general location. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience and collect usage data. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • As required to deliver our services (e.g., Google platform integrations)
  • As required by law or legal process
  • With your explicit consent

Data Retention

We retain client data for as long as necessary to provide services and fulfill legal obligations. Upon termination of services, client account access is revoked and personal data is deleted upon request.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at luke@titanadgrouphq.com.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
