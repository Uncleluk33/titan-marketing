Common Questions

What does a Google Ads audit actually include?

We review your account structure, keyword match types, wasted spend, Quality Score, and landing page alignment, then send you a clear write-up of what's working, what's leaking budget, and what we'd change first.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. Every engagement is month-to-month. You stay because the account is performing, not because you're locked in.

Who owns the Google Ads account?

You do. Your account, your data, your campaigns — we manage it on your behalf, but nothing is ever locked to Titan Marketing Agency.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

Local service businesses only — towing, plumbing, HVAC, electricians, contractors, and similar trades. That focus is why we understand your customers and keywords faster than a generalist agency.
